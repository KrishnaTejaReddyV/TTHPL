Founder's Desk

TTHPL is an organization pioneering into the realm concerning national interest making all efforts to tap the versatile talent (academics and sports) at grass root level. We are optimistic, that this humble and sincere initiative will provide platform to attract the best talent.

TTHPL firmly believe that Talent needs to be nurtured sincerely & smartly to deliver performance & excellence. The objective of a talent hunt is to spot the talent, expose him/her to competition & bench-marking to the world and making of ‘would be’ great personalities in their respective field steering the national cause. The ethos of the organization is based upon ‘serving the nation’ within the strict compliance of integrity and self-reliance.

TTHPL sincerely solicit your permission to witness the event being conducted at place. On receipt of confirmation, further modalities can be synchronized with your nodal officer and accordingly, we are willing to follow all procedures and documentations within the framework of your rules & regulations.

Sir, your positive response will go a long way in the furtherance of the cause of the nation as a whole. I also request you to kindly give wide publicity to this so that maximum students can be benefitted and prove to the world that:

“YES, WE CAN DO”

Thanks and Regards,
