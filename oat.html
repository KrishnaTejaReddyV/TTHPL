Olympiad Aptitude Tests (OAT)

OAT Paper Set OAT Results


Olympiad Aptitude Tests (OAT) is a comprehensive contest of Science, Maths, English and Logical reasoning on single platform to all students irrespective to caste, creed, religion and region to compete with each other at highest level to discover hidden talent treasure.

Eligibilty: Students of classes 1st to 10th are eligible to participate in this contest.


Particulars:

Last date of receiving filled application forms 12th Dec. 2016
Date of Examination 11th Jan. 2017

OAT Test Duration and Pattern:

  • The medium of test will be English
  • All questions will be of objective-type with no negative marking for wrong answers

Structure:

  • Offline
  • Contest will be conducted in the school premises of the respective schools

Questions Bifurcation:

  • English - 15 Questions
  • Maths - 15 Questions
  • Science - 15 Questions
  • Reasoning - 15 Questions

Knowledge / Skills Tested

  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Data Interpretation
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Conceptual Knowledge
  • Logical Reasoning, and Analytical Reasoning

Levels of Examination: OAT will conduct only one exam.

Test Answer Sheet: OMR Based (bubbles to be darkened by HB pencil/ black ball pen)

Syllabus: OAT question papers will be the combination of all boards.
